Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026

TraceWear is a Windows desktop professional prototype published by Jacob Brown. Core simulation, replay, capture import, analysis, Help, and report export operate locally and do not require internet access.

Information processed

TraceWear may process vehicle profiles, component reference libraries, diagnostic codes, recorded BUS captures, imported files, adapter status, and maintenance reports to calculate prototype estimates and prepare local reports.

Local storage

Imported data and exported reports remain on the user's device unless the user chooses to share them. TraceWear does not enable analytics or advertising telemetry by default.

Network use

Internet access is optional. TraceWear may check this repository for signed updates. Checks are non-blocking, and installation starts only after user confirmation. Optional support, submission, and synchronization actions may use the network when configured and deliberately used.

Bug reports are local by default. Never post sensitive vehicle, maintenance, personnel, organization, classified, controlled, or export-restricted information in a public issue.

Hardware

PCAN, Kvaser, and RP1210 integrations are preview paths. Vendor drivers are not bundled. Adapter information is processed locally unless the user exports or submits it.

Sharing and control

TraceWear does not sell personal information. Data is shared only through a user-chosen export, public support submission, or configured synchronization action. Users can work offline and remove local exported files.

Contact

Open privacy or support questions at TraceWear public support.
